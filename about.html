Who We Are

About Umul Qura

A trusted Islamic educational and community hub in the heart of Kitchener, Ontario.

Our Story

From a basement to a vibrant community centre

In 2011, Maalimah Zahra founded Umul-Qura Learning Centre in her basement. With a passion for teaching and Islamic knowledge, she started with a small group of students — addressing a significant gap in the Kitchener-Waterloo area by providing a dedicated space for Muslims to learn and grow.

What began as a grassroots initiative has now grown into a vibrant learning centre where education and spiritual growth go hand in hand. Today we serve children, youth, women, men, and families with a commitment to nurturing faith, knowledge, and character in a safe and inclusive space.

✦

Our Vision

To raise confident, compassionate, and spiritually grounded individuals who live by the teachings of Islam, serve their communities, and contribute positively to Canadian society.

✦

Our Mission

To provide authentic, accessible Islamic education and meaningful programs that strengthen faith, promote strong character, and build a sense of community rooted in mutual respect, love, and service. We aim to nurture the hearts and minds of our youth, support men and women in their spiritual journeys, and offer safe spaces where everyone can connect with their deen and with one another.

Why Choose Us

What makes Umul Qura different

  • Community-led: guided by values that reflect the needs and hopes of the people we serve.
  • Faith-rooted: every program is grounded in Islamic teachings.
  • Inclusive & safe: everyone is welcome — regardless of age, background, or knowledge level.
  • Experienced teachers: our instructors offer personal attention, encouragement, and mentorship.
  • Supportive environment: a place where learners feel seen, heard, and spiritually supported.
