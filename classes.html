Course Syllabus

Qa'idah Noorani & Tajweed Course

A structured, seven-level path from letter recognition to full mastery of Tajweed rules. Certificates are issued to students after the final test.

1Level One

By the end of this level the student has mastered:

  • Informal correct Makhaarij
  • Informal correct Huroof-e-Tafkheem
  • Informal correct Huroof-e-Tarqeeq
  • Identification of letters
  • Sukoon — application
  • Fatha, Dhamma, Kasra
2Level Two

By the end of this level the student has mastered:

  • Noon Mushaddad, Meem Mushaddad — application of Ghunna
  • Huroof-e-Tafkheem — application
  • Huroof-e-Tarqeeq — application
  • Laam of Allah — application
  • Sukoon, Tashdeed — application
  • Fatha, Dhamma, Kasra, Tanween, Fatha Muqaddarah, Kasra Muqaddarah, Dhamma Muqaddarah — application
3Level Three

By the end of this level the student has mastered:

  • Huroof-e-Halqi
  • Ghunna: Mutaharrik, Saakin, Mushaddad, Tanween
  • Madde Muttasil, Madde Munfasil — duration and application
  • Open and closed letters — long and short vowels
  • Basic rules of Waqf (e.g. last letter becomes a saakin when stopping)
4Level Four

By the end of this level the student has mastered:

  • Definitions of Tajweed, Sifat, Makhraj, Saakin, Tanween, Mushaddad and Ghunna
  • The rule of the letter Laam in the word Allah
  • The rule of the letter Raa Mutaharrik
  • The rule of Izhaar — complete
  • The rule of Idghaam — complete
5Level Five

By the end of this level the student has mastered:

  • The rule of Qalb — complete
  • The rule of Ikhfa — complete
  • The rule of Qalqalah — complete
  • The rules of Waqf — complete
  • First 15 letters of Makhaarij
6Level Six

By the end of this level the student has mastered:

  • The rule of the letter Raa — complete
  • Types of Madd — complete
  • Signs of Waqf (stopping)
  • Remainder of Makhaarij of letters
7Level Seven

By the end of this level the student has mastered:

  • Revision of all rules
  • Daily revision of Makhaarij
  • Final test — certificates are issued to students after the test

Hifdh Course

Children who read the Qur'an proficiently in Arabic and show a strong desire to memorize it join a dedicated program. Each student receives a daily lesson — from half a page for beginners up to three pages for advanced students. The program includes:

  • Qaida Noraani course
  • New lessons & review
  • Suba'c
  • Tafsir class
  • Islamic studies
